About Shirley

Shirley Ann Thompson (nee Parsons) died peacefully at home on June 25th, 2015 in Lincoln, California at the age of 83.

Shirley was born on December 14th, 1931 in Oakland, California to Leonard James and Ellsbeth Parsons. She is survived by her husband, Jerry Donald Thompson; children, Susan Angelique Kinney (Michael Kinney), Pamela Elizabeth Bertoli, and Gregory James Gentry (Susan Maischaider Gentry); grandchildren, Jessica Nicole Barry, Michael Webb Barry, Loralee Starla Gentry, Kathryn Marie Kinney, Alanna Marie Bertoli, and Roman Michael Kinney; nephew and niece, Steven James Parsons (Ginny Parsons) and Halley Nell Sanchez (nee Parsons) (Mark Sanchez); dogs, Gracie and Yoyo. She is preceded in death by her brother, Richard "Dick" Parsons, and daughter, Anne Marie Gentry.

Shirley was a dedicated and loving mother and grandmother. She raised three kids as a single parent in the Bay Area of California. She was an intelligent, independent, strong, and sweet woman who worked hard and loved fiercely.
