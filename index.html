About Shirley

Shirley Anne Thompson (nee Parsons) died peacefully at home on June 25th, 2015 in Lincoln, California at the age of 82.

Shirley was born on December 14th, 1931 in Oakland, California to Leonard James and Ellsbeth Parsons. She is survived by her husband, Jerry Thompson; children, Susan Angelique Kinney, Pamela Elisabeth Bertoli, and Gregory James Gentry; grandchildren, Jessica Barry, Michael Barry, Loralee Starla Gentry, Kathryn Kinney, Alanna Bertoli, and Roman Kinney; nephew and niece, Steve Parsons and Halley Sanchez, and dogs, Gracie and Yoyo. She is preceded in death by her brother, Dick Parsons, and daughter, Anne Marie Gentry.

Shirley was a dedicated and loving mother and grandmother. She raised three kids as a single parent in the Bay Area of California. She was an intelligent, independent, strong, and sweet woman who worked hard and loved fiercely.
